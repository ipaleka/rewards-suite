Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Rewards Suite, provided by ASA Stats ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when you access our website and use our services to submit contributions and claim rewards.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to verify your contributions and facilitate blockchain-based rewards.

A. Personal Data

When you interact with the Rewards Suite, we may collect the following:

  • Social Media Handles: Usernames and IDs from platforms including Discord, X (formerly Twitter), Reddit, and Telegram when you submit a contribution or suggestion.
  • Wallet Information: Your public blockchain wallet address (e.g., Algorand address) required to connect to the site and claim rewards.
  • Contribution Content: The text, links, and media contained in your bug reports, feature requests, or feedback.

B. Usage Data

We may collect information on how the Service is accessed and used, such as your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, browser version, and the pages of our Service that you visit.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following specific purposes:

  • Verification: To link your social media suggestions to your contribution profile and verify the validity of bug reports or feature requests.
  • Reward Distribution: To execute smart contracts that allocate and distribute rewards to your connected wallet address.
  • Transparency: To generate public transparency reports regarding reward allocations and project governance.
  • Communication: To notify you of the status of your contribution (e.g., via social media bot responses like emojis or comments).

4. Blockchain and Public Data

Important Notice Regarding Web3 Privacy

The Rewards Suite operates on public blockchain infrastructure (such as Algorand).

  • Immutability: Transactions made to claim rewards are recorded on a public ledger. Once data is written to the blockchain, it cannot be deleted or altered by ASA Stats.
  • Public Visibility: Your wallet address and the transaction history associated with our smart contracts are publicly visible. We cannot control how third parties interpret or use this on-chain data.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may use third-party tools for issue tracking (e.g., GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket) where your contribution data may be processed.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. However, please note that any data recorded on the blockchain is permanent and cannot be deleted by us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR for EU users, CCPA for California residents), you may have rights regarding your data held on our centralized servers, including:

  • The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.
  • The right to withdraw consent.

Note

These rights generally apply to off-chain data (like our internal databases) and do not extend to immutable blockchain records..

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Entity: ASA Stats
  • Email: support@asastats.com
  • Website: https://www.asastats.com
